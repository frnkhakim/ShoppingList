# Privacy Policy for My Shopping List

**Last updated: July 8, 2026**

## Overview

My Shopping List is a simple, offline shopping list app. We take your privacy seriously — and the simplest way to protect it is to not collect any data at all.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.**

My Shopping List does not:
- Collect your name, email, or any personal information
- Require an account or registration
- Track your location
- Access your contacts, camera, or microphone
- Send any data to external servers
- Use analytics or advertising SDKs
- Share data with third parties

## How Your Data Is Stored

All shopping list data (your items) is stored **locally on your device only** using your device's built-in storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or anyone else.

## Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that collect user data.

## Children's Privacy

This app does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Any changes will be reflected in the app's Play Store listing.
